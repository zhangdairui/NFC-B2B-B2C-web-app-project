About us

Welcome to NFC health

Newark Fulfillment Centers is a series of warehouses that services local small businesses who do not possess suitable space to hold / host their inventory. Just like Amazon Fulfillment Services, small businesses will send inventory directly to NFC to store, process and fulfill orders on behalf of these businesses. The types of businesses t hat it serves expands the spectrum from health and well being to medical supplies such as PPE all the way to dog / pet grooming and clothing. NFC i s a self-contained system that no outsiders are allowed into the facilities, and everything needs to be checked-in and checked-out through ownership accounts.
